"Hello! Fans of Ethiopian music"

A New Year, a New Sound: with Jano Concert Ethiopia

Celebrate the new year with the sounds of Ethiopia! Well-known diverse artists and organizers will present an astounding variety of performances, including traditional and contemporary Ethiopian music, dance, and theater.

2023.9.3(Sat) ~ 2023.9.4(SUN)

@Meskel Square, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Main Program

Traditional Music

Listen to the musicians from various cities about the music that they will be performing.

Exhibition

Appreciate various music pieces from jazz legends from the past and the present.

Forum

Take the time to talk with the musicians and express your ideas.

Modern music

Asking the jazz musicians any question regarding jazz related issues.

Ignite

Get opportunities to network with the jazz bands, also after the festival.
